# Bhasha-Kahani
AI-powered multilingual storytelling platform

Bhasha Kahani is a modern storytelling platform designed to preserve and share traditional folk stories through both text and audio formats. The platform allows users to explore cultural stories from different regions, listen to narrated tales, and experience storytelling in an interactive and user-friendly way.

The application focuses on creating an engaging digital experience with a clean UI, responsive design, and smooth navigation. It aims to promote regional storytelling, cultural preservation, and accessible entertainment through a simple and visually appealing web platform.
